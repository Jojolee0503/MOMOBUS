Teemo's Orgin

On a snowy day on February 25, 2019, a star 🌟 was born. The second this majestic animal was born the sky stopped snowing
the gloomy sky faded away and a gleam of sunlight shined direct at this newborn baby dog. Different from his siblings
when Teemo was pushed out of his mother's womb he was not pink, slimy and ugly but instead he comes out
glowing in warm yellow-orange light and in a body of the rich luscious brown coat, a pair of beady eyes and splendid body size and dimension.
He was perfect in all sorts of ways. (Legend said that the light emitting from Teemo was so strong that the nurse who was in charge of the operation was blinded. )
Shortly after, Teemo stood up and let out his first woof and it was at that moment the world stops everything they were doing and listen to his harmonic
chime-like bark. And there is the orgin of Teemo Murciélago Agiu Gucci Lee.
